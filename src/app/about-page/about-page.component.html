About GradLink

GradLink is your ultimate guide to navigating the post-graduation journey! GradLink offers University of Florida graduates an exclusive platform to connect with fellow alumni, share experiences, and gain valuable insights. From personalized resume reviews to joining alumni groups in new cities, GradLink equips graduates with the resources to navigate their careers and build meaningful connections. Whether seeking mentorship, exploring job opportunities, or engaging with people in the same position, GradLink is your gateway to thriving beyond graduation.

How to Get Started

No account? No problem! Creating one is quick and easy. Simply click Sign Up and complete the required fields. Choose a username, write a short bio, and share any social media links you would like to include. Once done, select your graduation date and major, and you will be directed to your personalized chat room, tailored just for you.
